Open Source Ecology

Buying some land or renting a space and use it to build open source construction and agriculture machinery, that we then use to build an aquaponics greenhouse and some utilities and habitable buildings. We can organise workshops, design and build larger buildings, etc.

The steps

  • Buying some land (as a cooperative) in the village or its outskirts;
  • Building Open Source Ecology machinery for agriculture and construction;
  • Building structures based on the work of OBI: aquaponic greenhouse, small living units, small utility units;
  • Growing food following permaculture principles (vegetable garden, aquaponics greenhouse, chicken, fly production, mushrooms, etc);
  • Producing as much as possible of our energy;
  • Organising workshops on how to do all of the above;
  • Optional: designing and building more things, such as multiple floor apartment buildings (that depends on our funds, on legal matters, on what members want to do, etc);
  • Pptional: selling fair construction services.

Minimum amount of people: 6, among which several people with enough time to dedicate at least 2 full-time equivalent (FTE).

Skills required:

  • Ability to use tools and build relatively complex machines (min 1 FTE),
  • Ability to deal with heavy machinery (min 0.5 TBD),
  • Ideally: engineering skills
  • Ideally: architect
  • Ideally: legal counsel

Commitment level: low (helping a bit here and there) to high (in total at least 2 FTE with specific skills required for several people for the project to be valid; long term investments required).
