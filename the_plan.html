Building Reset Society

Living better and sustainably can be done in several ways. Our objective targets various aspects of life that can be improved, which form the pieces of the puzzle that we need to put together to achieve our goals.

Some of these pieces can represent a lot of work, but they can be broken down into tiny pieces that do not require a lot of resources. That means that if you are interested in what we do and want to participate but think that don’t have the time or money or ideas to help, you are probably wrong. There are lots of ways to participate, even with very little time or resources. You can read more about that in our our blog, and learn about the pieces of the puzzle below.

  • Surroundings

    Living in a place with a lovely weather, good food and quick access to awesome regions.

  • Working better

    Less stress, less commute, more oportunities to improve professionally.

  • Food & Energy

    Using ecologically and mostly locally produced food & energy.

  • Social enterprises

    Promoting companies with social goals and ethical backgrounds.

  • Tech innovation

    Living in a place where technological innovation thrives.

  • Your ideas

    Your ideas, it is impossible to come up with a working model without them.

  • Surroundings

    We spent several months selecting the perfect place to start this project until we finally chose Rincon de la Victoria. We ended with a place with:

    • Great weather
    • Awesome food
    • A great beach
    • Good housing prices
    • Close to mountains, forests and snow
    • Close transport hubs

  • Working better

    We want to promote ways to work that result in a better quality of life and profesional development.

    We are organising and participating to a number of projects to promote:

    • Coworking space(s)
    • Talks and workshops
    • Reduced commute
    • Conferences
    • Fab Labs, such as this one
    • And more to come…

  • Food & Energy

    Using ecologically and mostly locally produced food & energy:

    • Consumming locally produced food
    • Consuming food with a low environmental footprint
    • Producing our own food using permaculture techniques and aquaponics
    • Consuming 100% renewable energy
    • Producing our own renewable energy
    • And more to come…

  • Social enterprises

    Promoting companies with social goals and ethical backgrounds:

    • Buying goods and services from ethical providers
    • Actively participating in networks supporting social enterprise
    • Stimulating cooperation between social actors
    • Offering advice and help to social enterprises
    • Boosting the development of social enterprises
    • Developing circular economy systems in the region

  • Tech innovation

    Living in a place where technological innovation thrives, which we trigger with:

    • Talks and workshops
    • Hackathons
    • Accelerator programmes
    • Coworking space(s)
    • Testing, using and promoting innovative solutions
    • Knowledge exchange communities
